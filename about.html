About our History

Our Success Story

We Started

In 2018, GK FOODS LIMITED was founded as a rural-based agribusiness company located in Busesa Town,Bugweri District,Eastern Uganda. Founded with intent of processing grains for human consumption such as Rice, Beans, Soya, Groundnuts, Millet, Sorghum and Maize.

Results of Hardwork

Officially became a registered company in 2020, we grew into a diversified business with more than 8 products in our company portfolio. We own mills, pre-cleaning, grading and packaging machinery to enable us offer high quality products to our clients. 

Mission

“ To be the leading provider of essential daily food needs in Uganda through the operation of a profitable and thriving agro-processing business, thereby improving the living conditions of farmers from whom we source the raw materials ”.
